Who We Are

Building Tomorrow's AI Pioneers

The LPU School of AI & Emerging Technologies is a premier research and teaching institution dedicated to advancing the frontiers of artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, and data science. Located within the Lovely Professional University campus in Phagwara, Punjab, we nurture India's next generation of AI innovators.

Our interdisciplinary approach combines rigorous theoretical foundations with hands-on project-based learning, industry collaborations with global leaders like Quantiphi, and a state-of-the-art research ecosystem.

Admissions 2026-27

Your Journey Starts Here

Follow these steps to secure your place in India's premier AI school.

STEP 01 · MAY 2026
Register for LPUNEST

Complete online registration at admission.lpu.in and pay the application fee. Choose your preferred AI program.

STEP 02 · JUNE 2026
Appear for LPUNEST Exam

National Entrance and Scholarship Test covering Physics, Mathematics, Chemistry, and Aptitude. Online and offline modes available.

STEP 03 · JULY 2026
Merit List & Counselling

Check your results, participate in online counselling, and select your branch preference. Scholarship amounts announced.

STEP 04 · AUG 2026
Document Verification

Submit original Class 10, 12 marksheets, transfer certificate, and complete the admission formalities on campus or online.

STEP 05 · SEP 2026
Welcome to LPU AI School! 🎉

Orientation week, lab tours, faculty meet-and-greet, and the official start of your AI journey begins here.
